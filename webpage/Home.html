About Us

Malligai Mens Hostel is a premier accommodation facility located in the heart of Chennai, Tamil Nadu. We offer clean, spacious, and well-furnished rooms for working professionals and students. The hostel is equipped with modern amenities and 24/7 security for a safe stay. High-speed Wi-Fi and regular housekeeping services are available. Located near major bus stops, railway stations, and IT parks. Nutritious vegetarian and non-vegetarian meals are served daily. Common areas include a TV lounge, study room, and laundry zone. Power backup and purified drinking water ensure uninterrupted comfort. Affordable pricing with flexible sharing options (single/double/triple). Experience peaceful living in a homely and disciplined environment.
